The Problem

AI agents (like SAP Joule) are increasingly making autonomous procurement decisions.

The Risks:

  • Unapproved high-value purchases
  • Blocked vendors getting inadvertently re-engaged
  • No audit trail for AI reasoning
  • No "Kill Switch" when the AI hallucinates

The Solution: Aegis.ei

A middleware layer sitting between the AI Agent and the ERP.

AI Agent ➜ Aegis.ei (Risk Engine) ➜ ERP System (S/4HANA)
  • Intercepts AI-generated Purchase Orders.
  • Scores risk based on complex heuristics.
  • Dynamically assigns a tiered autonomy level.

Tiered Autonomy Model

Tier Description Use Case
T0 : Manual AI disabled. Human action only. Blocked vendors, overrides.
T1 : Suggest AI recommends, human decides. High risk, new vendors.
T2 : Co-Decide AI prepares, human confirms. Medium risk, above limits.
T3 : Auto-Execute AI executes independently. Low risk, routine orders.

System Highlights

  • 🔴 The Kill Switch: Instantly downgrades all AI actions to T1.
  • 📊 Composite Risk Scoring: Evaluates amount, vendor, category & seasonality.
  • 📋 Accountability Ledger: Immutable audit trail of every human/AI decision.
  • 🧠 Enterprise Intelligence Loop: (See next slide)

🧠 Enterprise Intelligence Loop

Aegis.ei learns from human overrides.

  • If humans consistently override T3 POs from "Vendor X", the ML model notices.
  • It dynamically recommends adjusting the Auto-Approve Thresholds.
  • Powered by scikit-learn Logistic Regression under the hood.
