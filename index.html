T0 System

T0 Manual Only

AI is completely disabled. All decisions made by humans. Use: sensitive negotiations, blocked vendors, legal holds.

T1 Suggest Mode

AI analyzes and recommends. Human must manually approve, modify, or reject. AI has no execution power.

T2 Co-Decide Mode

AI prepares the action for human confirmation. Decision queues with a countdown. Human confirms or overrides.

T3 Auto-Execute Mode

AI evaluates all rules and auto-approves if conditions are met. Notification sent. Routine, low-risk POs from trusted vendors.
